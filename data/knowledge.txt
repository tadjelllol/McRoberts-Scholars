McRoberts Scholars is a club dedicated to helping students find and apply for scholarships.

Important Information:
1. Club meetings are held every Wednesday at 3:30 PM in Room 201
2. Students can join our Discord server at https://discord.gg/j8SP6zxraN
3. We help with scholarship applications, essays, and interview preparation
4. Executive members update scholarship information regularly
5. Resources include meeting recordings, application guides, and essay templates

Scholarship Process:
1. Browse available scholarships on our website
2. Check eligibility requirements
3. Prepare required documents
4. Submit applications before deadlines
5. Attend workshops for application tips
